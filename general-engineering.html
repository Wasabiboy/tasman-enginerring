General Engineering and Fabrication

Our qualified welders are experts in both light and heavy fabrication – we pride ourselves on the variety of materials and sizes that we can fabricate. Whether it be a one-off product or a large production run of welded product using jigs and fixtures, we can help you.

Our welding capabilities include:

  • Mig, Arc and Tig Welding
  • Light and heavy fabrication
  • Custom jigs and fixtures
  • One-off products and production runs
  • Various materials and sizes

We work with a wide range of materials including Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper, Aluminium, and more. Our team has the expertise to handle projects from small components to large assemblies.

Tasman Engineering provides complete engineering solutions. From design to prototyping, then full production - we can do it all in house.
