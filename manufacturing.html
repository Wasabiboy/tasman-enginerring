Manufacturing Services

Metal Pressing Services… Shaping Your Future

Our metal pressing plant produces products in Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper and Aluminium. We are able to develop products from prototype, production tooling to finished product – we can do it all in house.

We have ten mechanical power presses with capacity from 5 to 200 tonnes. Several of the power presses have very large platen dimensions allowing for pressing of larger components. Our press options include automated coil feed and air cushions for drawing operations.

CNC Turning

Our precision turning and machining operation manufactures a comprehensive range of components using the latest in CNC equipment. We are able to achieve per part savings using the most effective machining practices possible.

Our services include:

  • CAD/CAM
  • Precision machining
  • CNC turning
  • 2 and 3 axis. 2 to 4 axis
  • Bar feed components
  • Billets and castings
  • CNC milling

Tasman Engineering provides complete engineering solutions. From design to prototyping, then full production - we can do it all in house.
